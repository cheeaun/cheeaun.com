Goodbye 2009, Hello 2010

1 January 2010

It's time for a short review of what I did for the past few months in the year 2009.

Let's do an unordered list here:

  • Those who don't follow my tweets should know that I'm now living and working in Singapore. I moved out from Kuala Lumpur and started working in Singapore by around August. Migrating was quite a tiring but fun exercise, especially to another country. I find it very convenient to travel around the places in Singapore with its good transportation system, more organized directions and online maps.

  • Ever since then, I've been joining a number of events such as GeekCamp Singapore, Singapore Google Technology User Group meetups, WebSG meetup, BarCamp Singapore 4, and Neoteny Singapore Camp 1. I did a short lightning talk, a presentation plus demo, another awesome demo, and a 5-minute talk. Wow. The good news is I get a lot of freebies from those events. Awesome.

  • Last year, I had 3000 tweets. Now, it's a whooping 12000 tweets! That's a huge increase by 400%!

So, that's all. My new year resolution perhaps would be to update this blog as often as possible. Or better yet, tweet more often! Haha!

Happy New Year 2010 to everyone.
