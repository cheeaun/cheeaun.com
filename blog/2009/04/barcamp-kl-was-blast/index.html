BarCamp KL was a blast!

7 April 2009

Last weekend was really a blast, thanks to the BarCamp KL event held on April 4th and 5th. This is my second time participating in BarCamp and it just got even better than I thought. I met with few familiar faces like:

  • Nazrul (@nazroll)
  • Andy Goh (@hantu)
  • Ikhwan Nazri (@tekong)
  • Zi Bin from Opera (@zibin)
  • Colin (@bytebot)
  • Khai Lee (@khailee)
  • Mike Foong (@mikefoong)
  • @Zeo
  • and more...

Plus few new faces like:

  • Marvin Lee who is Nazrul's Hackathon buddy (@marvinlee)
  • Philip Chee who is the guy behind all the cool Firefox extensions
  • Gen Kanai from Mozilla (@gen)
  • Ben Scherry (@jfxberns)
  • @31o5
  • and few more guys from somewhere in Malaysia, Singapore and Cambodia.

There were few sessions that I attended and really like, such as:

  • Browser War 2: Standards strikes back
  • Ikhwan Nazri: Your Office is everywhere - Mobile Office, which explains a little on how to be motivated by so-called distractions in Starbucks.
  • Hackintosh BarcampKL
  • a presentation about jamiQ: Social Media Intelligence on Emotional Web.
  • Kufi Squares Barcamp Kl
  • How To Date A Japanese Girl and Episode 2
  • Twit Bots made in Japan
  • and one more presentation by some guy about his adventure climbing up the Kilimanjaro. Totally rocks.

What a blast.
